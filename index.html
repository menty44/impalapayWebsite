IPPF CHAT APP

This is an Android based chat application with possibility of anonymous login and registration. The application enable users exchange textual messages in the chat room as well as take photos from their phones of incidences that occur in their locality and be prompted to give the location of the photos for quick/fast processing.

The chat application also allows the creation of multiple groups based on either location or issues being discussed. Users are able to join and leave groups at will when using the appliacation.

TotoHealth

We use mobile technology to help reduce maternal and child mortality and detect developmental abnormalities in early stages.

Totohealth enables mothers and fathers to receive targeted and personalized messages timed at their child's age or stage of pregnancy. These messages are able to highlight any warning signs in a child's health/development, equip you with knowledge on nutrition, reproductive health, parenting and developmental stimulation.

UAP GROUP – AFYAIMARA

AfyaImara app gives you the ability to access health insurance from UAP wherever you're on your android device. You can calculate premiums, learn more about our health cover plans, view our provider panel, register and get covered. UAP - Better.Simple.Life.

M-Afya | Malteser International

M-Afya is a Maternal Neonatal Health (MNH) project that works with 10 private health facilities within Nairobi informal settlements to provide pregnant women with a flexible financing plan for maternity. This plan allows pregnant women to make pre-payments over the period of pregnancy for maternity fees using lipa na M-pesa services.

Ojay Greene

Ojay Greene is a platform that help smallholder crop farmers get their produce to profitable urban markets by utilizing a sms system that advises farmers on quality of seed, land optimization and offers them support through Agronomists who ensure higher better quality yields, thereby increasing their competitiveness and income levels.
